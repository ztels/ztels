Variation flows
First-time vs. repeat user
Intro
First-time: full onboarding card. Repeat: "Welcome back — pick today's Duo."
Pairing
First-time: friend list from scratch. Repeat: last Duo surfaced first with "Pair again with Maya?"
Prompt
First-time: timer shown. Repeat: streak counter shown ("Day 3 🔥").
Settings
First-time: feature tips visible. Repeat: clean toggle view only.
Success vs. error states
Pairing
Success: "You're paired with Maya!" → auto-advance. Error: "Couldn't connect. Try again later." + retry CTA.
Post
Success: "You both posted! 🔆" Error: "Post failed. Your photo is saved — tap to retry."
Pending
One-sided post: "Posted! Waiting on Maya…" with live indicator.
Opt-out
"Pairing removed. You can always rejoin later." → returns to standard feed.
